About Me

I am Amber Green of Stone + Colour Creative. As a full time Graphic Designer, I created this company as a hobby where I could be hands on in the creative process. There has always been within me a desire to create.

Growing up as a woodworker's daughter I was always watching my father in his element creating pieces of his trade. Over the years my father taught me how to use the tools and machines in his shop.

I started to create my own pieces by reconstructing wood and the materials my father no longer needed for his job. I took pride in reclaiming and reviving these scraps that would otherwise be tossed away to create beautiful, modern, everyday decor for everyone's home or office.
